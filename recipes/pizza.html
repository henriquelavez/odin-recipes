Little Itamar's Pizza Dough

Pizza Dough

This is the recipe of the best pizza dough I have ever tasted, created by my friend Itamarzinho Caetano.

Ingredients

Quantity: 4 pizzas

  • 1 kg wheat flour
  • 20 gr dry yeast
  • 150 ml oil
  • + or - 500 ml of barely heated water
  • 2 generous pinches of sugar
  • 2 standard pinches of salt

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients
  2. Add everything else and knead the dough for aproximately 10 minutes
  3. Let the dough rest until it doubles it's size
  4. Invite who gave you this recipe for dinner
